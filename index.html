HAPPY HANUMAN JANMOTSAV

Hanuman Janmotsav is celebrated to remember the Hindu God, ‘Mahabali Hanuman’. This day is dedicated to Lord Hanuman as he was born on this day. People worship Lord Hanuman and offer prayers to the lord. This day is also celebrated as Hanuman Janmotsav."Wishing you a happy Hanuman Jayanti, filled with blessings, peace, and prosperity. May Lord Hanuman's blessings be with you always."
